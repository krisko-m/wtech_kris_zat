Game Description

Bang! is a frantic card shootout for 4-7 players that will take you on an excursion to the Wild West! Irresistible "Spaghetti Western" atmosphere and constant action. At the beginning, players choose a gunslinger whose character they will play and draw roles. You can become a sheriff or his assistant or, conversely, you will stand on the side of evil as a bandit or even a renegade who fights against everyone!
Bang! is a typical "party game", the fun is mainly in surprising twists, the result is not paramount. The game includes talking, whether bluffing, chatting with opponents, comments on exceptionally successful actions of teammates, or other serious and non-serious topics unrelated to the game. The game itself is fast-paced and so rarely ends after the first game...
Compared to other games in this category, Bang! is a livelier game, even with seven players there is still something happening. There are practically no boring passages in the game. The brilliantly conceived game principle gives the opportunity for surprising twists. The Italian card names and the gunslingers inspired by famous characters from the Wild West (the names are humorously garbled) nicely illustrate the atmosphere of the game. Simply Bang! is a good game.

Gameplay

Each player has 3-4 lives depending on which gunman from a wide selection (e.g. Calamity Jane, Jesse Jones, El Gringo, Black Jack) they represent, the sheriff has one more life. The player who drew the sheriff card publicly signs up and starts the whole game. The roles of the others remain secret. In the first rounds, it is still not very clear who is who. But as soon as the bandits (who have only one goal - to eliminate the sheriff by any means) start shooting, the situation becomes clear. But it is not that easy, only the one who can shoot at the sheriff or anyone else can shoot at him. And at the beginning of the game, you just hit the players sitting next to you with your small gun.
The two basic cards in the game are Bang! and Mancato!. Bang! symbolizes shooting. During one turn, a player can play only one Bang! card. However, there are exceptions, which are described in the rules. The second (Mancato!) means that the shooter missed and is of course a necessary reaction of the attacked player. If your opponents have already taken some life, you will be happy to play the Beer card, which will give you one life back (sic!). There are fewer other cards in the deck, but they are very appropriately chosen and dramatize the game wonderfully. It often happens that a gunslinger in a seemingly hopeless situation unexpectedly "comes to life" thanks to lucky cards, and on the contrary, a player who feels almost immortal dies within one round!
